# Salary System (Current Understanding)

This section needs validation from client.

## Wage Rate

Company uses daily wage rate.

Example:

₹700/day

Salary Calculation:

Wage Rate × Payable Days

Example:

₹700 × 26 days

= ₹18,200

---

## Higher Than Minimum Wage

Company may pay more than minimum wage.

Example:

Minimum Wage = ₹700/day

Actual Wage = ₹800/day

Need clarification on terminology used.

---

## PF (Provident Fund)

Current understanding:

PF can be enabled/disabled employee-wise.

Example:

PF Wage = ₹15,000

Employee PF:

12% = ₹1,800

Employer PF:

12% = ₹1,800

Total Contribution:

₹3,600

Need confirmation from client.

---

## Bonus

Need clarification.

Possible types:

* Festival Bonus
* Performance Bonus
* Special Bonus

---

## Overtime

Need clarification.

Possible methods:

### Method 1

Fixed amount per hour.

Example:

₹150/hour

### Method 2

Hourly rate based.

### Method 3

Double-rate calculation.

Need actual company process.

---

## Salary Increment

Must maintain history.

Never overwrite salary.

Example:

01-Jan-2026 → ₹700/day

01-Jul-2026 → ₹750/day

01-Jan-2027 → ₹800/day

System should calculate salary according to revision effective date.
